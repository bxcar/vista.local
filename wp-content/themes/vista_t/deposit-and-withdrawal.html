Important note:

Vistabrokers CIF Ltd accepts direct payments made by account’s holder only:

  • Wire transfer made by account’s holder from its bank account; and / or
  • Payment made by account’s holder debit / credit card.
  • All transactions are provided by PSP Kalixa Pay LTD. Kalixa Pay Ltd. is authorized by the Financial Conduct Authority under the Electronic Money Regulations 2011 (register ref: 900005) for the issuing of electronic money.
  • Bank, Cards or other alternative payment systems – all transfer fees are covered by the Client

Warning: Vistabrokers CIF Ltd doesn’t accept payments made by / through the third party!
